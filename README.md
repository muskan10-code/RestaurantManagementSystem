The "Restaurant Management System" is a complete program made to maximize and simplify
the functioning of restaurants. This system is designed to meet the various needs of restaurant
employees by providing them with effective tools that improve business management and
provide guests with outstanding dining experiences. With three different user roles—Admin,
Staff, and Customers—the system makes sure that every user has a smooth and customized
experience.

Key Features:
1. User Management: Secure sign-up, login, and profile updates for Admins, Staff, and
Customers.
2. Menu Management: Easy creation, editing, and browsing of restaurant menus by Admins
and Customers.
3. Order Management: Efficient order placement, modification, and cancellation, along with
reservation handling.
4. Billing and Payment: Seamless generation of bills and processing of payments within the
system.
5. 6. Order History: Convenient tracking of previous orders for quick reference and reordering.
Feedback and Complaints: Ability for Customers to leave reviews and complaints,
enabling service improvement.
Objective:
The system's goal is to completely transform restaurant management and customer service with
its intuitive interface and extensive capability.

Scope of the Project:
The "Restaurant Management System" project focuses on developing a versatile software
solution for the hospitality industry. With functionalities like order processing, inventory
management, and staff administration, the system caters to the needs of restaurant owners,
customers, and staff. It allows administrators to efficiently modify menus and oversee
operations, while empowering staff to handle orders and billing tasks seamlessly. By staying
agile and responsive to industry trends, the software aims to enhance dining experiences for
patrons and streamline restaurant operations, ultimately boosting efficiency and customer
satisfaction in a competitive market.
